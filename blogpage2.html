Sweet rolls

Dessert where you use meter more than flour

Pff, this is the recipe where I can always improve something.
I never ever though about making sweet rolls until I got one from my ex. Call it destiny, call it madness but I fell in love. Also a bit with her. From that time I make sweet rolls regulary, they have plenty of good sides. They are easy to make, you can fill them with anything, they are soft and most importantly, they are sweeet! Very easy to make, from the moment we bought mixer. You just add too much yeast for the volume, other tingredients from the recipe and then extra flour because dough is always sticky and recipe is short of approximately 2 cups of flour. Wait two hours, great lenght if you are preparing lunch for the family then just fill it up, roll it without spilling filling on the sides, cut it and lay the pieces like babies to their bed. Bake a bit just make sure its not dry, either not wet. Take it out ann lay it with snow-sugar coating. The thing I need to work on because I don't like the taste and basicness of the icing. And its done hands clean and family happy because you were so great and made the dessert also.
