Sweet rolls

Dessert where you use meter more than flour

Pff, this is the recipe where I can always improve something.
I never ever though about making sweet rolls until I got one from my ex. Call it destiny, call it madness but I fell in love. Also a bit with her. From that time I make sweet rolls regulary, they have plenty of good sides. You can fill them with anything you find in the kitchen, they are soft and most importantly, they are sweeet! Very easy to make, from the moment we bought mixer. You just add too much yeast for the volume of the flour, other ingredients from the recipe and then extra flour because dough is always sticky and recipe is always short of approximately 2 cups of it. Wait two hours, great lenght if you are preparing lunch for the family, then just fill the dough, roll it without spilling filling all over your work space, cut it and lay the pieces like babies to their bed. Bake a bit just make sure its not dry, nor too wet. Take it out and lay it with something, anything from pudding to snow-sugar coating works. Crazier the better. This is the thing I need to work on because I don't like the taste and basic look of the icing and I am not too patient when I take the rolls out. And its done, hands clean and family happy because you were so great and made the dessert alongside the main meal.
